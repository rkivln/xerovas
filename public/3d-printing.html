QUESTIONS

FAQ

What file formats do you accept?
We primarily accept .STL, .OBJ, and .3MF files. For industrial SLS/Metal, we can also process .STEP and .IGES files via manual quote.
What is your maximum build volume?
Our current maximum build volume for FDM is 500mm x 500mm x 500mm. For high-resolution Resin, it is 190mm x 120mm x 200mm.
How long does shipping take?
Standard orders are processed in 3-5 business days. We offer Express 24-hour turnaround for Professional and Industrial tiers.
